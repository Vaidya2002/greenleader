Our Mission: Education, Environment, Empowerment

Pratibimb

  1. Home
  2. PRATIBIMB

e Even though India is one of the fastest developing countries, its vast population possesses a variety of problems for a majority of the people. Necessities become luxury which sets generations back to decades and hinders their development. And for these people, education becomes their only hope. But this basic right of education, this necessity, has now become a luxury. Over 30 million children in the country do not attend school, and the pandemic has worsened the situation even more. These numbers are daunting, but there's always something we can do. To make education more accessible and to encourage more children and families towards quality education, we started Pratibimb

Education is the foundation upon which we build our future.

-Christine Gregoire

Starting with Nagpur in Ashok Nagar with around 40+ children and since 2021 Pratibimb has expanded its horizon currently standing with 400+ children with Sakkardara, Rameshwari, Indira Nagar, Shivaji Nagar, Fire college and Kamptee as the new locations with more to come. Apart from academics, we also try to give students a glimpse of other co curricular activities as a whole creating a school-like environment. This encourages and enables every child to become an independent thinker and self-learner. The motive behind Pratibimb is to make learning joyful, provide quality education with all the required amenities, and help transform the community
